Our Story

Baked with care,
brewed with intention.

Ember & Grain started with a simple conviction: a good café should feel like the best part of your day. Not just the coffee — the light through the windows, the smell of something coming out of the oven, a face that already knows your order.

We bake everything in-house, every morning. Our coffee rotates through three small-batch roasters we've actually visited. And our menu changes with the seasons, because that's just how food should work.
